About SkyFightPlus

SkyFightPlus is your ultimate personal music and video shuffle player. Upload two songs and an ad/video from your PC. The player will shuffle the songs and insert the ad/video at the interval you set. Pause, skip, and visualize your audio with neon waveforms.
